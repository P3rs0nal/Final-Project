Some Simple Beats

A drum beat is what provides rhythm and structure to most music. Typically, beats are arranged using different drum sounds in a repeating pattern.

  • Step: A single sound from a drum (shown below as a numbered column). For the example below, the beat is divided into 12 steps.
  • Measure/Bar: A group of beats. Typically, the most common measure is 4 beats. The measures are shown within the small dividers.
  • Time Signature: Determines how many beats are in a measure.
  • Tempo: The speed of the beat, measured in BPM (beats per minute). The higher the BPM, the faster the beat. The example below is set to a BPM of 120. With all DAWs, you are able to adjust the BPM to your liking, but for this example we are using the standard tempo for most songs.
