Some Simple Beats

A drum beat provides rhythm and structure to most music. Beats are arranged using different drum sounds in a repeating pattern.

  • Step: A single sound from a drum (shown below as a numbered column). The beat is divided into 12 steps.
  • Measure/Bar: A group of beats. The most common measure contains 4 beats.
  • Time Signature: Determines how many beats are in a measure.
  • Tempo: The speed of the beat, measured in BPM (beats per minute). This example uses a standard tempo of 120 BPM.
